## 8. Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time.

**How to delete your account**: Go to **Settings → Account → Delete Account** within the app.

Upon account deletion, the following data will be removed:
- Profile information (email address, display name)
- Your tasks and routine data
- Your association with team data
- Your push notification token

Please note that shared content associated with a team (data collaboratively created by team members) may be handled differently. Please review the in-app information before deleting your account.
